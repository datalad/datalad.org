DataLad is a distributed and collaborative data management system supporting arbitrarily large datasets, on-demand content retrieval, dataset nesting, data version control, provenance capture, and metadata management. DataLad development is supported primarily by the US National Science Foundation and the German Federal Ministry of Education and Research.
